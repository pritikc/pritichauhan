Relox Virtual Lab

Mobile App Design

In collaboration with Relox Virtual Labs, I embarked on a transformative journey to revolutionize healthcare diagnostics through intuitive user experience design.

The project aimed to enhance the accessibility, accuracy, and efficiency of pathology reporting, ultimately speeding up the diagnosis and treatment process for patients worldwide.

  Project Summary

Designed and Developed a suite of mobile applications tailored to revolutionize healthcare management, encompassing pathology, center operations, phlebotomy, and patient engagement. Each app addresses specific needs within the healthcare ecosystem, from streamlining diagnostic processes and laboratory operations to enhancing patient care and accessibility. Through intuitive interfaces and robust features, the project aims to optimize efficiency, improve communication, and empower both healthcare professionals and patients in their respective roles.

Design Process
Phase 1
Empathize

Understand the user problem through User Interviews and Research Expert opinions

Phase 2
Specify

Analyze Findings, identify goals and prioritize features based on findings

Phase 3
Ideate

Create low fidelity wireframes to explore design posibilities

Phase 4
Test

Test the design against users to gather feedback and ensure it solves a problem

Phase 5
Design System

Creating the Tone, Color Palette, Iconography and Typography of the brand

Phase 6
Finalize

Based on feedback from users, generate final designs for the mobile and desktop app
